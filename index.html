TALES OF KINIS SONNTO

The Unyielding Knight

Peace - is it every truly achievable? Maintainable? How long?

Thousands of years after the Battle of Yavin, a new era of peace flourishes across the territories that are governed by the Galactic Republic.

The Jedi Order has bolstered their numbers and send their students along with their masters to the farthest reaches of the known galaxy to peacefully resolve conflicts.

A Jedi master sits down with his apprentice, a newly christened Jedi Knight. The peace that is maintained throughout the galaxy was not always the how it was. The mentor begins to tell the eager student a tale from his past from when he was a Jedi Knight himself.

From a time when peace was more fragile and the missions the Jedi High Council bestowed upon the infamous Jedi knights would take them down a more perilous path...
