THE UNYIELDING KNIGHT

Peace! Thousands of years after the Battle of Yavin, the SITH ORDER rose again to spread fear into the galaxy. Through the heroic acts of the Jedi knights, the Sith were routed.

This ushered in a new era of peace across the territories that were governed by the GALACTIC REPUBLIC. The JEDI ORDER now have bolstered their numbers and send their students along with their masters to the farthest reaches of known space to peacefully resolve conflicts.

In the Jedi temple on Coruscant, a Jedi master sits down with his apprentice - a newly christened Jedi Knight - inside the Room of a Thousand Fountains. The peace that is maintained throughout the galaxy was not always this way. The mentor begins to tell the eager student a tale from his past.

From a time when peace was more fragile and the missions undetaken by the brave Jedi knights were perilous...
